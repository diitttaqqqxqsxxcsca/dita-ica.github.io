The Future is Now

"School of Media and IT Mini Conference in AI"

Discovering
AI

Explore the diverse applications of AI, the challenges and opportunities that come with its development, and how it can be leveraged to make a positive impact on society. We look forward to engaging in meaningful conversations that will further the progress of AI and its potential to shape the future.

Explore AI with the Experts Experts

Our conference features an impressive list of experts from various fields of AI. They will be sharing their knowledge and insights about the industry's latest trends and future possibilities.

Mark Anderson

AI Research Scientist

Mark is a seasoned expert in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). He has spent the last decade delving into the nuances of AI, and exploring its possibilities.

Sarah Johnson

Healthcare Expert

Sarah is a healthcare professional with an immense interest in the application of AI in medicine and health services. She has been part of various innovative projects that have revolutionized patient care through AI. In her talk, Sarah will share insights on how AI is being utilized in healthcare, the progress so far, and what the future holds.

Jennifer Williams

Machine Learning Expert

Jennifer is an accomplished Machine Learning expert. She has worked on multiple projects that have harnessed machine learning for a variety of applications. In her session, she will discuss the fundamental aspects of Machine Learning, its potential uses, and how it's shaping industries.

Andrew Smith

NLP Specialist

Andrew is a linguist and computer scientist, making him the perfect blend for the realm of Natural Language Processing (NLP). Andrew's discussion will center on NLP - its significance, applications, and the role it plays in making AI more 'human-friendly'.

Rebecca Jones

Future AI Analyst

Rebecca is a visionary futurist with a deep understanding of AI. She has been analyzing the role of AI in shaping our future. Her talk will delve into the potential future of AI, its implications for society, and the opportunities and challenges that may arise as AI becomes increasingly prevalent in our lives.
